## Concepts: "─Бva┼Ыyakam pr─Бptam pary─Бptam - Necessary Obtained Sufficient"

## Learning
"─Аva┼Ыyakam" means "necessary", "pr─Бptam" means "obtained", and "pary─Бptam" means "sufficient" in Sanskrit. These are adjectives that describe the state or quality of something. They are used to express necessity, acquisition, and adequacy.
