## Concepts: "kriy─Бpad─Бni ba.va. - Verbs Plural"

## Learning
"Kriy─Бpad─Бni" means "verbs" and "ba.va." is an abbreviation for "bahuvacanam" (plural) in Sanskrit. This section teaches how to use verbs in plural forms. Understanding verb conjugation for different numbers is essential for proper Sanskrit grammar.
