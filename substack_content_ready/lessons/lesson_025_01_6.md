# Title: 1.2.6 This masculine feminine neuter - eс╣гaс╕е eс╣г─Б etat - рдПрд╖рдГ рдПрд╖рд╛ рдПрддрддреН
## Concepts: "eс╣гaс╕е eс╣г─Б etat - This (all genders)"

## Learning
In Sanskrit, "eс╣гaс╕е" means "this" for masculine nouns, "eс╣г─Б" means "this" for feminine nouns, and "etat" means "this" for neuter nouns. These demonstrative pronouns change according to the gender of the noun they refer to.
