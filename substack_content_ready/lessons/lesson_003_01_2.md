# Title: 1.1.2 What is Your name? feminine - Bhavaty─Бс╕е n─Бma - рднрд╡рддреНрдпрд╛рдГ рдирд╛рдо
## Concepts: "Bhavaty─Бс╕е n─Бma kim"

## Learning
In Sanskrit, to ask "What is your name?" to a female person, we use "Bhavaty─Бс╕е n─Бma kim?". "Bhavaty─Бс╕е" is the respectful form of "you" for females, "n─Бma" means "name", and "kim" means "what". This is the polite way to ask a woman's name.
