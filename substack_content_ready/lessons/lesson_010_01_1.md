## Concepts: "pr─Бrthan─Бr┼лp─Бс╣Зi ─Бj├▒─Бr┼лp─Бс╣Зi - Forms of requests Forms of commands"

## Learning
"Pr─Бrthan─Бr┼лp─Бс╣Зi" means "forms of requests" and "─Бj├▒─Бr┼лp─Бс╣Зi" means "forms of commands" in Sanskrit. These are imperative forms used to make requests or give commands. Understanding these forms is important for polite and direct communication.
