# Title: 36.1 Although Yet - yadyapi tath─Бpi - рдпрджреНрдпрдкрд┐ рддрдерд╛рдкрд┐
## Concepts: "yadyapi tath─Бpi - Although Yet"

## Learning
"Yadyapi" means "although" and "tath─Бpi" means "yet" in Sanskrit. These are concessive particles used to express contrast or opposition. Understanding concessive particles is essential for expressing contrast and opposition in Sanskrit.
