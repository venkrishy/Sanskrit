# Title: 22.1 As much as so much - y─Бvat t─Бvat - рдпрд╛рд╡рддреН рддрд╛рд╡рддреН
## Concepts: "y─Бvat t─Бvat - As much as so much"

## Learning
"Y─Бvat" means "as much as" and "t─Бvat" means "so much" in Sanskrit. These are correlative words used to express quantity and extent. Understanding correlatives is essential for expressing comparisons and measurements in Sanskrit.
